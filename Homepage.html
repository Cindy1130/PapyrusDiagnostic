About Papyrus

When your cells get injured, they release small fragments of DNA called circulating cell-free DNA (cfDNA) into your blood stream. This molecule comes in many different types, where each can provide specific information on organ injury, infections, and transplant rejections. Papyrus is a company with a proprietary paper-based test that measures cfDNA and generates a colorimetric output. The Papyrus Color Visualizer reads and interprets the intensity of this color to correlate it to cfDNA levels, in addition to tracking it and sending it to a designated health provider. Papyrus color scanner is looking to expand to reading the colorimetric output generated by other paper-based diagnostic tests currently on the market. The mission of Papyrus diagnostics is to bring rapid, paper-diagnostic tests that act as diagnostic and longitudinal monitoring tool to patient homes.
